The Thesis

AI is breaking careers. We are here to fund what comes next.

The moment a Meta or Google engineer stops optimizing someone else's roadmap and the hackathon champion stops building demos, that is where OnlyExit lives.

We Only Back Frontier Problems

Every company we back has to own a model no frontier lab can copy. Proprietary data, or a model trained on data the product keeps collecting. Not on day one. But on a clock, and the clock is running.

Regulation x AI

The data is walled off. Trust, compliance, and liability keep the frontier labs out, and that wall is what gives a startup room to accumulate. AI can already do the job. Permission is the bottleneck, not the model. Payments. Healthcare. Finance. Privacy infrastructure.

Capability x AI

The model does not exist yet. Not a wiring problem, an invention problem. Someone has to build the intelligence, not just call an API. New architectures. New interfaces. New behaviors.

If a frontier model can ship your product as a feature update next Tuesday, this is not the place for you.

Consumer can be frontier. Regulation and capability are just the two reasons a data moat is possible at all. If you launch fast enough and collect enough before anyone blinks, the sector does not matter.
