The Thesis

The Startup Ecosystem Invests in Founders. We Create Them.

World-class infrastructure exists for companies that already exist. VCs fund them. Accelerators refine them. Nobody builds infrastructure for the stage before that. The moment a senior engineer at Amazon, a product lead at Meta, or a systems architect at Google wakes up thinking about escape.

That moment is where OnlyExit lives. We create environments where high-signal operators collide, explore hard problems, test founder compatibility, and launch startups.

01

The Talent Flood

Amazon, Meta, Google, and Block have cut thousands of senior operators. Principal engineers, product leaders, engineering managers who ran systems serving hundreds of millions of users. They have the skills, networks, and runway to build companies. They need a starting point.

02

AI Changed the Math

What previously required 20 engineers now takes 3. The barrier to starting has never been lower for people with the right skills. AI enables early-stage teams to take on problems that used to require 50 engineers.

03

The Culture Shifted

The safety net that kept elite builders inside Big Tech is fraying. Another FAANG offer, another RSU package, another cycle inside the machine. Operators increasingly want ownership, not employment.

We Only Back Frontier Problems

Integration Frontier

AI can theoretically do the job. But the world hasn't built the trust layer, the compliance layer, or the liability chain to let it. Healthcare. Finance. Infrastructure.

Systems Frontier

AI cannot do this today. These problems require highly innovative thinking for AI to even begin making inroads. Defense. Deeptech. Construction. Supply chain.

If Claude can ship your product as a feature update next Tuesday, this isn't the place for you.
